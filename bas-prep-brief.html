ATO Reference

What the codes actually mean

You don't need to memorise these. Cowork applies them. But it helps to know what's being sorted.

G1
Total Sales
All GST-taxable income. The invoices you sent with GST on them. 10% of this becomes 1A.
GST collected: 10%
G10
Capital Purchases
Equipment, computers, tools over $300. These are assets, not just expenses. May need a depreciation schedule.
GST claimable: 10%
G11
Operating Expenses
Software, rent, contractor fees, professional services. Everything that keeps the business running day to day.
GST claimable: 10%
1A
GST Collected
What you owe the ATO. Automatically 10% of your G1 sales. Your accountant calculates this from what you report.
Auto-calculated
1B
GST Credits
What you can claim back. 10% of G10 and G11, but only when the supplier has an Australian ABN on their invoice.
Auto-calculated
